Tool chain for C/C++

* git/github
* Hello World programs in C and C++
* editors and IDEs
    * Old School editors.  
         * examples: emacs, vi/vim
    * Full on IDEs. 
         * examples: XCode, Eclipse, Netbeans, CLion
    * Middle ground: 
         * examples: Sublime Text, Notepad++
* gcc, clang, g++ and clang++ compilers
* make and Makefiles
* various kinds of files: `.c`, `.h`, `.cpp`, `.hpp`, `.o`, executable
* debugger: gdb
